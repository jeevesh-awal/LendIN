Peer to Peer smart lending




ABOUT US

LendIn is a secure peer to peer lending platform that enables its users to lend and borrow money according to their needs. The lenders and the buyers are both given equal beneficial opportunities.

LendIn uses a transparent, compliant and effective system wherein the lender is able to increase/decrease their percentage of interest in accordance with the other lenders by viewing live updates about the latest transactions that have taken place. We give a similar beneficial opportunity to the borrowers by which they can request their desired percentage of interest in accordance to the current market scenario.

FAQ's



Frequentely asked questions

1. Why LendIn?

  • Continuous live updates on the percentage of interest.
  • Freedom to choose your percentage of interest according to the live market scenario.
  • Transparent, compliant and effective interface.
  • No middleman.
  • Fully secure process.
  • Free registration.

2. Who can be a borrower?

With the exception of borrowing for illegal or prohibited purposes under Indian law, individuals can borrow for any purpose. The borrower can be:

  • Any potential SME who is willing to start, grow and expand their business.
  • People adhering to the policies and code of conduct at LendIn.
  • Person having an annual income of more than 3 lakhs.
  • Aadhaar card and PAN card holder.

3. What are the documents required?

  • 2 passport sized Photographs
  • PAN Card
  • Aadhaar Card
  • Voter ID
  • Passport
  • Last 3 month’s bank statements
